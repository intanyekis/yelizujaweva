    Barbara Broccoli is an American film producer with a net worth of $300 million. She was born on the 18th of June in the year 1960 in Los Angeles, California in the United States of America. The source of Barbara Broccoli net worth is mostly by her investment in the film industry as a producer. In the 2008 New Year Honors, Barbara Broccoli was appointed Officer of the Order of the British Empire by the Queen of United Kingdom.

    She is the daughter of Albert R. “Cubby” Broccoli (Producer of the Famous movie James Bond) and Dana Wilson Broccoli (An Actress). At the age of 17, she started working in the Bond franchise. Six years later, she became assistant director for "Octopussy". After working for "Octopussy", she became an associate producer on "The Living Daylights". In the year 2012, she co-produced James Bond films with Michael G. Wilson (her half-brother). She has produced in many films like "GoldenEye" (1995), "Die Another Day" (2002), "Quantum of Solace" (2008) and the most recent Bond Film: "Skyfall" (2012).

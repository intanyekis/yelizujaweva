    Although there was speculation Leonardo DiCaprio and Gigi Hadid’s romance had fizzled out, the two are apparently still involved. According to one report, the duo continues to see each other but are free to date other people.

    RELATED: How Gigi Hadid Has Worked To Protect Her Daughter Khai Hadid Malik From The Public Eye

    Leo & Gigi Reportedly Have An Open Relationship, But She May Not Be Happy

    Leonardo and Gigi first sparked romance rumors in September 2022 when they were spotted spending time together in New York on multiple occasions. However, by February, it was reportedly over. “Gigi and Leo are no longer dating,” a source said at the time. “Things just organically tapered off between them. [The twosome] were at different points in their life, and it just didn’t work out between the two of them.”

    Leo was seen spending time with a variety of different women in the aftermath. Most recently, he was linked to model Irina Shayk.

    But it appears Leonardo and Gigi have found a way back to one another. According to a new report, the pair are casually seeing each other, though they’re avoiding labels. “Leo and Gigi have a friendly, no-strings ‘situationship’ right now,” a source told Us Weekly. “[They] travel so often so it’s best for them to keep their relationship open and fluid. Neither one of them wants to settle down at the moment.”

    RELATED: Gigi Hadid's Net Worth Outshines Her Sister Bella's, But How Much Does She Actually Make Modeling?

    Leonardo is clearly taking advantage of his and Gigi’s open relationship. The Titanic star was recently photographed partying with a group of models – an activity he does often.

    Leo’s reputation as a womanizer doesn’t make the no-strings attached situation surprising for him, but it is unusual for Gigi. The model has a history of long-term relationships. She was with her daughter’s father, Zayn Malik, on-and-off for nearly a decade before they seemingly called it quits for good in 2021.

    Some reports have suggested Gigi is interested in something more serious with Leo. She may have reluctantly agreed to an open relationship with him if she felt that’s the only way they could keep seeing each other.

    Leonardo Is Known For Dating Women Even Younger Than Gigi Hadid

    Gigi is 27-years-old, but that’s quite a bit younger than Leo, who’s 48. However, he’s garnered a reputation for not dating women under the age of 25. He was even recently linked to a 19-year-old Israeli model Eden Polani, which his team swiftly denied.

    Last year, Leonardo broke up with model Camila Morrone shortly after her 25th birthday. The Internet responded with tons of jokes about how Camila was too old for him. Shortly after, Leo and Gigi became romantically involved. But there was speculation it was all a big publicity stunt. Since Gigi is slightly older than the women Leo normally dates, it was suspected it may have been a move to improve his reputation after his break-up with Camila.

    They may still not be seriously dating, but Leonardo and Gigi clearly have a connection, given that they rekindled their flame. But with Leo’s dating history and Gigi’s commitment to her career as well as her daughter, only time will tell if this unexpected celebrity relationship will work out in the long-run or not.

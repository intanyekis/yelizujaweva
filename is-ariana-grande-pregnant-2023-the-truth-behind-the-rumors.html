But is there any truth to these rumors? 

    The Positions Album and Music Videos

    The rumors about Ariana Grande’s pregnancy started after she released her sixth studio album, Positions, in October 2020. The album features lyrics and visuals that could be interpreted as being suggestive of a pregnancy or a desire to have a baby. For example, in the song “34+35”, Ariana sings “Can you stay up all night?/F–k me ’til the daylight”, which some fans have taken as a reference to conception. She also wears a white dress that resembles a wedding gown, implying that she is ready to settle down and start a family.

    Another factor that fueled the rumors about Ariana Grande’s pregnancy was her secret wedding with Dalton Gomez, a real estate agent whom she started dating in January 2020. The wedding was attended by only 20 guests, mostly close family and friends. Ariana looked stunning in a strapless silk dress by Vera Wang, accessorized with pearl and diamond earrings by Lorraine Schwartz. The rumors are based on assumptions and interpretations that are not confirmed or denied by Ariana or her team. Therefore, it is best to respect Ariana’s privacy and not spread false or misleading information about her personal life. Ariana has always been vocal about her love for music and her fans, and she deserves to enjoy her married life with Dalton without being subjected to unnecessary speculation. If and when Ariana decides to share any news about her pregnancy or her plans to have a baby, we will be happy to celebrate with her.

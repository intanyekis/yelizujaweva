    Abby Elliott is an American actor, voice artist, producer, writer, and comedian. She was a cast member on Saturday Night Live from 2008 to 2012. Some of her film and television roles include playing Rebecca Klein in the NBC sitcom Indebted, Brooke Von-Weber in the Bravo comedy series Odd Mom Out, Joy in the romantic comedy film No Strings Attached, Jeanette Peterson in the CBS sitcom How I Met Your Mother, Chelsea in the animated series Home: Adventures with Tip and Oh, Vanessa in the romantic comedy-drama film Life Partners, Monica in the improvised comedy film High Road, Bree/Joselyn in the sketch comedy series Inside Amy Schumer, Janna (voice) in the animated magical girl television series Star vs. the Forces of Evil, and Taylor in the superhero film Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

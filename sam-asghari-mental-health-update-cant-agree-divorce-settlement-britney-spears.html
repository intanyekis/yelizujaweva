    Sam Denied That He Wants To Date Again

    Sam declined to discuss his estranged wife directly, but he admitted that he’s done a lot of growing throughout recent years, which he says is what’s brought him to his current stage of life.

    Sam And Britney’s Prenup Could Entitle Him To Nothing

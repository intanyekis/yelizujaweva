    Thanks, but no thanks! Teen Mom star Jenelle Evans told The Ashley that she turned down a sex tape offer from Vivid Entertainment. Evans' confession comes following rumors that fellow MTV star Mackenzie McKee is reportedly battling Vivid over the release of her own tape.

    Thanks, but no thanks! Teen Mom star Jenelle Evans told The Ashley that she turned down a sex tape offer from Vivid Entertainment. Evans' confession comes following rumors that fellow MTV star Mackenzie McKee is reportedly battling Vivid over the release of her own tape.

    “They contacted my lawyer and wanted to offer me $1.5 million,” Evans reportedly told the site. “I told my lawyer to respond for me and say, ‘No thank you, not interested but flattered.’ My future and reputation is more important than gaining a quick buck. And it’s against my morals as a person. That’s just so disrespectful to yourself.” 

    The troubled reality star recently told Us Weekly that she’s focused on graduation and then gaining back custody of her son Jace.

    21 May 2024, 00:00

    The wedding bells are ringing! Janice Dickinson married her longtime love, Dr. Robert “Rocky” Gerner, on Saturday, December 10, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

    The supermodel, 61, and the Los Angeles–based psychiatrist tied the knot in an intimate wedding ceremony at the home of the bride’s friend Suzan Hughes in Beverly Hills, according to Entertainment Tonight. "It was such a fun celebration," the insider tells Us.

    Dickinson looked gorgeous in a white Jane Booke gown. "I think you need for your wedding, some statement dress, I really do," she told ET. "And I have the perfect one."

    As the happy couple exchanged their vows, the talent agent broke down in tears as she thanked guest Dr. Drew Pinsky for helping her with her sobriety, ET reported. Later, the newlyweds danced to their wedding song, "You Don't Own Me," performed by Crown Jwlz.

    "I am so delighted, ecstatic, over the moon, out of body, a runaway bride," Dickinson told the news outlet. "When Rocky first introduced himself to me, I felt like I had seen a unicorn, a one of a kind. He's the brightest and best and the funniest and most exciting man I've ever met in my life."

    During the wedding reception, guests dined on a gourmet meal prepared by Joey Santos of Joey's Food, according to ET. The evening's dishes included grilled chicken skewers with spinach almond pesto, mini quiches and various tea sandwiches and salads. The pastel-colored three-tiered wedding cake featured three decadent flavors: red velvet, chocolate fudge and vanilla.

    Earlier this year, the couple, who exclusively announced their engagement to Us Weekly in December 2012, were forced to briefly place their wedding planning on hold after Dickinson was diagnosed with breast cancer. "I used to obsess over image. I'm the world's first supermodel," she said during an April 6 appearance on The Doctors. "I've been in front of cameras. I will never keep quiet when something is bothering me. I'm a fighter."

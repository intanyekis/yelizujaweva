    Who is Brad Bufanda?

    Fred Joseph Bufanda III or as we all know, Brad Bufanda was an up and rising Hollywood actor who came to fame following his appearance in Veronica Mars.

    Not only was Brad an actor, but he was also a social media star as he also received limelight from his self-made internet videos.

    What Caused Brad Bufanda’s Death?

    The late actor Brad Bufanda died on Wednesday, November 1, 2017, at age 34 in LA, California, USA. The cause of his death? Well, it was apparently suicide!

    In fact, Bufanda jumped off of his Park La Brea apartment home in LA. To be exact, he supposedly bounced off of the 13th floor.

    Following his demise, Brad’s rep showed her grief,

    We are completely devastated for he was an extremely talented young actor and wonderful, caring human being. He was reviving his career having just completed two movies and we are shocked and saddened by his passing.

    According to authorities, Brad left a suicide not thanking his parents and other important people in his life. As for the reason for his suicide, many claim it to be his declining acting career as he used to post about such on his socials.

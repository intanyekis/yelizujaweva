    Tristan 'Mack' Wilds' Wedding Photo with Wife Looks Hilarious as Their Baby Cries on the Side

    Actor Tristan 'Mack' Wilds posted his wedding photo on Instagram and all his fans zoomed in on their daughter's hilarious reaction. &quot;Shots Fired&quot; star Tristan 'Mack' Wilds recently reminisced the time he married his long-time love, Christina Hammond, amid the coronavirus pandemic.

    27 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Actor Tristan 'Mack' Wilds posted his wedding photo on Instagram and all his fans zoomed in on their daughter's hilarious reaction.

    "Shots Fired" star Tristan 'Mack' Wilds recently reminisced the time he married his long-time love, Christina Hammond, amid the coronavirus pandemic.

    The 31-year-old actor took to Instagram to share one of the snaps taken during their wedding last year which featured his daughter, Tristyn Naomi, stealing the spotlight.

    A DRAMATIC BABY

    In the photo, Wilds, who looked dapper in an all-black suit, planted a kiss on Hammond, who was holding a large bouquet. She dazzled in a green lace dress and nude high heels.

    While the "90210" alum gushed over his picture with his forever lady, their adorable daughter Tristyn was featured in the photo with a hilarious reaction while their parents shared a romantic kiss.

    The little girl, whom they welcomed in May 2020, wore a checkered dress with a red ribbon and shoes. Many fans noticed that Wilds' daughter didn't seem amused by the situation as she appeared to be crying under her kissing parents.

    A lot of Wilds' friends and fans sent their warm wishes to him and Hammond on the online platform. But they couldn't resist laughing at their daughter's hilarious expression.

    One fan commented that Tristyn was cute while in tears and that the toddler will surely laugh hard upon revisiting the photo in the future. Another fan wrote:

    "This photo is everything for so many reasons! Congratulations!"

    HER FAVORITE LOVE STORY

    Wilds and Hammond tied the knot on December 17, 2020, in an intimate ceremony. Since large crowds have been highly discouraged due to the pandemic, their only guests during their special day were their parents through Zoom.

    Wilds was charged with unlicensed operation of a motor vehicle and unlicensed driving.

    A fan's comment on Tristan "Mack" Wilds' picture saying that the snap is "everything." | Photo: instagram.com/mackwilds

    The couple is living proof that love is timeless as they have been together since they were 15. For the "The Wire" star, marrying the woman he has known for a long time is a fairy tale come true.

    For Hammond, it has been her dream to marry Wilds too. Though it took a long time for them to become husband and wife, she shared that their relationship is her "favorite love story."

    Last year was a dark moment for Wilds. The police pulled him from his 2017 Jeep Wrangler in New York City for having dark-tinted windows and driving with a suspended driver's license.

    Wilds was charged with unlicensed operation of a motor vehicle and unlicensed driving. The charges were formally suspended after he settled the situation in court.

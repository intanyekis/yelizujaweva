    Early Life

    Kelsey Grammer, whose full name is Allen Kelsey Grammer, was born on February 21, 1955, in Charlotte Amalie, U.S. Virgin Islands. He was raised by his mother and maternal grandparents in New Jersey and Florida.

    Grammer attended the private preparatory school, Pine Crest School, and later earned a scholarship to study at the Julliard School. However, he was expelled from Julliard for failing to attend classes.

    Grammer faced personal tragedies in his early life, including the kidnapping and murder of his sister in 1975, the murder of his father in 1968, and the death of his two half-brothers in a scuba diving accident in 1980.

    Who is Joe Tippett?

    Joe Tippett is an American actor who is famous for his role in NBC’s American drama series ‘Rise’ as Sam Strickland. He also has played in films and performed many stage production.

    How old is Joe Tippett?

    Joe Tippett was born on March 1, 1982, in Washington, D.C., U.S. His current age is 38. There is no further information about his parents or his education as he hasn’t revealed it yet.

    Joe Tippett – Professional Career

    Being fond of acting since an early age, Tippett landed his first role in Ben in the movie ‘Gray Mattes’ in  2006. The movie was an American romantic comedy film directed by Sue Kramer. It premiered at the Hamptons International Film Festival on October 21, 2006.

    However, he did not appear in any other movies or projects after his first movie until 2011, where he became a part of the documentary called ‘Doctors of the Dark Side’.

    The documentary showcased the role of physicians and psychologists in the torture of prisoners. It mainly focuses on the covered-up signs of torture at the Guantanamo Bay Detention Camp and Abu Ghraib Prison. The following year, Tippett appeared in a short film called ‘Damascus Roads’ as Finch.

    In 2013, Tippett made his debut in Television series called ‘Boardwalk Empire’ where he played the role of Cal. It is an American period crime drama series that was set chiefly in Atlantic City, New Jersey. In the same year, he performed in a stage play called ‘Ashville’ as Jake.

    In 2015, he appeared in another  American crime thriller television series called ‘The Blacklist’ Jasper Skeehan. He also performed in a musical called ‘Waitress’ as Earl Hunterson.

    In 2016, he appeared in both film and TV series. He played Jamie in the movie ‘Our Time’ and he played Merle Deavers in the TV series ‘Bull’. Likewise, he also appeared as Brad in a play called ‘Familiar’ which was written by Danai Gurira.

    In 2017, he played the role of Jake Benjamin in the legal drama television series called ‘Chicago Justice’. Unfortunately, NBC canceled the show after the first season on May 22, 2017. In the same year, Tippett performed in a theatre called ‘All the Fine Boys’ as Joseph.

    The following year, he performed in a movie called ‘Monsters and Men’ as Officer Montori. He also appeared in a TV series called ‘Dirty John’ as Bobby.

    In 2019, he played the role of Doolittle Lynn in the movie ‘Pasty and Loretta’. Furthermore, he appeared in three different TV series in the same year which was ‘The Act’, ‘Patsy & Loretta’, and ‘The Morning Show’.

    When it is time to tune in to the chart-topping melodies of country music, there is no other person to turn to than Jason Aldean, one of country music's biggest stars! Born on February 28, 1977, this talented crooner has serenaded audiences with his heartfelt lyrics and magnetic stage presence. From the infectious Dirt Road Anthem to the anthemic My Kinda Party, each track on this list showcases Aldean's unmistakable voice and his ability to captivate listeners. In this piece, we'll present to you the top 10 highest-grossing Jason Aldean songs, ranked by the number of copies sold worldwide. So, grab your dancing boots, turn up the volume, and let's vibe to the irresistible world of Jason Aldean's greatest hits!

    Take a Little Ride - 2012 (4 Million Units)

    Via Flickr/Country4you

    Released as the lead single from his album Night Train, Take a Little Ride serves up a hefty dose of country-rock goodness. Produced by Michael Knox, it hits the gas with its driving beats and Aldean's dynamic vocals. This song quickly became a fan favorite, revving up the charts and dominating the airwaves. With its infectious energy and adrenaline-fueled lyrics, this track has taken listeners on a thrilling journey, selling a solid 4 million units worldwide.

    Fly Over States - 2012 (5 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Fly Over States was released as the fifth single from his album My Kinda Party. This song pays homage to the heartland and the often-overlooked beauty of flyover states. Produced by Michael Knox, it combines Aldean's distinctive voice with heartfelt lyrics, painting a vivid picture of small-town America. Fly Over States resonated with listeners, capturing the pride and resilience of everyday people with its relatable message and Aldean's emotive delivery, selling an impressive 5 million units worldwide.

    Night Train - 2012 (6 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Jason Aldean's high-energy title track Night Train, released as the second single from his album of the same name delivers a thrilling ride of country-rock goodness. Produced by Michael Knox, it showcases Aldean's powerful vocals and the band's electrifying instrumentation. Night Train became a fan favorite, capturing the exhilaration of a wild night out. With its infectious energy and memorable chorus, this track has steamrolled its way to success, selling a rocking 6 million units worldwide.

    Big Green Tractor - 2009 (7 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Aldean's feel-good country tune, Big Green Tractor was released as the second single from his album Wide Open. This song combines catchy melodies with playful lyrics, painting a picture of simple pleasures and rural charm. Produced by Michael Knox, it became a radio favorite, resonating with listeners across the globe. With its irresistible hooks and Aldean's laid-back delivery, Big Green Tractor plowed its way to success, selling 7 million units and solidifying its place in Aldean's repertoire.

    She's Country - 2009 (8 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    She's Country, released in 2009 as the lead single from Jason Aldean’s album, Wide Open celebrates the fierce and independent spirit of country women. Produced by Michael Knox, it packs a punch with its electrifying guitars and Aldean's signature vocals. She's Country became an instant hit, capturing the hearts of fans with its high-energy sound, and selling a rocking 8 million units worldwide.

    You Make It Easy - 2018 (10 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Released as the lead single from his album Rearview Town in 2018, You Make It Easy is a testament to the power of love. Produced by Michael Knox, it showcases Aldean's emotive vocals and vulnerable lyrics. With its captivating melody and poignant storytelling, You Make It Easy struck a chord with listeners worldwide, selling an impressive 10 million units, and making it a standout in Aldean's discography.

    Burnin' It Down - 2014 (15 Million Units)

    Via Flickr/Taste of Country

    Jason Aldean's steamy track, Burnin' It Down was released as the lead single from his album Old Boots, New Dirt in 2014. Produced by Michael Knox, this song sets the mood with its seductive lyrics and sultry melodies and showcases Aldean's smooth vocals and irresistible charm. Burnin' It Down became an instant hit with its irresistible blend of country and R&B influences and it has burned its way into the hearts of fans worldwide, selling a scorching 15 million units.

    Dirt Road Anthem - 2010 (20 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Dirt Road Anthem was released in 2010 as part of Jason’s album My Kinda Party. Produced by Michael Knox, this song is a perfect blend of country and hip-hop, taking listeners on a wild ride through rural landscapes. It features the smooth rap stylings of Colt Ford and it quickly became an anthem of summer road trips and good times. This chart-topper has sold a staggering 20 million units worldwide, proving its timeless appeal.

    Got What I Got - 2020 (22 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Released in 2020 as the second single from his album 9, Got What I Got showcases Aldean's tender side and his gratitude for a supportive partner. It combines heartfelt lyrics with a soothing melody, creating an uplifting and soulful experience. This song resonated with fans, becoming a sentimental favorite and selling an impressive 22 million units worldwide. It is a testament to Aldean's versatility as an artist and his ability to touch the hearts of his audience.

    My Kinda Party - 2010 (25 Million Units)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Jason's electrifying anthem, My Kinda Party was released as the title track of his album. Produced by Michael Knox, this song delivers a high-octane performance that's impossible to resist. With its infectious hooks and sing-along chorus, My Kinda Party became a massive hit, selling a whopping 25 million units worldwide. This song is a celebration of life, music, and making memories, making it a staple in Aldean's live performances and a fan-favorite party anthem.

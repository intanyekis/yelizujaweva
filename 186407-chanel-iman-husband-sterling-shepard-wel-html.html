    Chanel Iman and Sterling Shepard now have two beautiful children after welcoming their daughter Cassie Snow into their lives right on time for the holidays.

    Supermodel Chanel Iman was once named one of the top 30 models of the 2000s by Vogue Paris, and while she continues to have some gigs here and there, she's decided to focus a lot of her time on starting a family with New York Giants wide receiver, Sterling Shepard.

    Now, their family received an early Christmas gift this year, as they grew by yet another member for two consecutive years.

    Chanel Iman and Sterling Shepard attending a formal event together | Source: Getty Images/GlobalImagesUkraine

    THE ARRIVAL OF THEIR SECOND DAUGHTER

    Their newborn daughter Cassie Snow Shepard was born on December 17, 2019, and they announced the news on Instagram by sharing an adorable family portrait of all of them on the hospital bed.

    Things have been moving fast for the family, as after getting married in March 2018, they welcomed their first daughter in August 2018.

    DOTING ON HER TWO BABY DAUGHTERS

    On another photo, the doting mom carries both her babies while on the hospital bed, savoring the new blessing she has of taking care of two children.

    Her firstborn Cali is seen with a huge smile on her face, clearly ecstatic over the arrival of her new baby sister.

    WELCOMING THEIR FIRST CHILD IN AUGUST 2018

    Things have been moving fast for the family, as after getting married in March 2018, they welcomed their first daughter in August 2018 through a normal delivery.

    At the time, Chanel's fans were made aware of their daughter's arrival in the same way, through an Instagram picture on a bed with her husband and their daughter Cali Clay Shepard sleeping peacefully on her chest. Of her daughter's arrival, she said:

    "You were worth every push every contraction! Welcome to our world [Cali Clay Shepard] Love mommy and daddy [Sterling]"

    CELEBRATING THE HOLIDAY SEASON WITH A NEW BUNDLE OF JOY

    After giving birth on December 17, that gives both Chanel and Sterling enough time to adjust to being a family of four, and ultimately, celebrate the coming holiday season with a new bundle of joy.

    Congratulations, Shepard family!

    How do I cancel subscriptions?

    Make sure to sign in to the Google Account that has your subscriptions.

    Manage your subscriptions on Google Play

  • Open the Google Play app .
  • At the top right, tap the profile icon.
  • Tap Payments & subscriptions. Subscriptions.
  • Select the subscription you want to cancel.
  • Tap Cancel subscription.
  • Follow the instructions.

    How can I delete activity log?

    Delete your activity automatically

    On the top left navigation panel, click Data & personalization. Under “Activity controls,” click Manage your activity controls. Below “Web & App Activity,” “YouTube History,” or “Location History,” click Auto-delete. Confirm to save your choice.

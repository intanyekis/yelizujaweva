    Dwyane Wade Trolls Terry Crews After He Tries Apologizing To Gabrielle Union

    Dwyane Wade has spoken up in his wife Gabrielle Union's defense after the actress was challenged by Terry Crews, who publicly refuted heraccusations of racist behavior on the America's Got Talent set.

    26 May 2024, 00:00

    Dwyane Wade has spoken up in his wife Gabrielle Union's defense after the actress was challenged by Terry Crews, who publicly refuted her accusations of racist behavior on the America's Got Talent set.

    Crews, who worked on the show too, recently knocked Gabrielle's claims back, suggesting she was either wrong or lying.

    "I can't speak for sexism because I'm not a woman, but I can speak on any racism comments," he said on the Today show last week. "That was never my experience. In fact, it was the most diverse place I have ever been in my 20 years of entertainment."

    The backlash came swiftly.

    Fans were upset at the fact that Terry would attempt to rebuff Gab's assertions before NBC made the findings of their own investigation public. His statement followed Union getting taken off the judging panel for the show back in November. Variety reported Union was ousted after making claims of sexism and racially insensitive behavior, including being told on countless occasions that her hairstyles were "too black."

    Crews has since apologized for his actions, admitting that he did not take time to consider anyone else's experience but his own.

    “I hear you, I respect you and understand you," he wrote on Twitter. I am sorry, and I am here to support you. I spoke from my own personal point of view without first taking into consideration someone else’s experience.”

    The Brooklyn Nine-Nine actor also applauded Gabrielle, branding her "a role model to the entire black community".

    “I informed @KevinHart4real a while back, he required 2 ‘acknowledge the discomfort of other individuals’ Now, I have to do the very same thing," he added. "I wish to excuse the remarks I made. I recognize there are a great deal of Black women injured and pull down by what I stated and likewise by what I didn’t state.”

    Gabrielle has not responded to Crews' apologies but several Twitter users have expressed their feelings on the matter and don't seem to believe that the 51-year-old was genuine.

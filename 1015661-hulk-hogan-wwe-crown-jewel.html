    05 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Let me tell you something, brother—Hulk Hogan is now officially being advertised as one of the featured talents at this Friday’s WWE Crown Jewel event in Saudi Arabia.

    WWE.com has updated the event listing and shows Hulk Hogan pictured as one of the ‘Featured Superstars’ for WWE Crown Jewel along with some of the talent competing in matches including DX, The Brothers Of Destruction and the WWE World Cup competitors. This is one day removed from Daniel Bryan getting written off the card by losing to AJ Styles on SmackDown in an impromptu WWE Championship to kick off the show. You can see Hogan’s featured picture (which now also features Samoa Joe) by going here

    Hogan has mentioned before that he was going to go on this week’s trip to Saudi Arabia, but WWE hasnt given any confirmation—until now. Hogan told the Orlando Sentinel that he was “looking forward to going to Saudi Arabia with the WWE, looking forward to WrestleMania” and said plans were moving rapidly, but he was excited to have this opportunity again after being away from the company.

    Hogan had been removed from the WWE Hall Of Fame due to his controversial remarks caught on camera, but was reinstated earlier this summer and appeared backstage at WWE Extreme Rules to speak with talent.
